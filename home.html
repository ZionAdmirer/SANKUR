About My Hobby

Reading biographies of influential tech personalities like Steve Jobs and Bill Gates ignites my curiosity and fuels my ambition. These narratives not only highlight their remarkable journeys but also offer profound lessons on resilience, creativity, and the transformative power of vision in shaping the technology landscape.

Benefits of My Hobby

  • Gain insights into the lives of successful individuals.
  • Learn about the challenges and triumphs in the tech industry.
  • Inspiration for personal and professional growth.

My Idols

Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs, the visionary co-founder of Apple Inc., revolutionized the tech industry with his innovative approach to design and functionality. His relentless pursuit of excellence led to groundbreaking products that changed the way we interact with technology.

Bill Gates

Bill Gates, co-founder of Microsoft, is a pivotal figure in the software industry. His foresight in personal computing has not only transformed the digital landscape but also positioned him as a leading philanthropist dedicated to improving global health and education.

Recommended Biographies

  • Steve Jobs by Walter Isaacson - An authorized biography that delves into the life of the co-founder of Apple, showcasing his vision and creativity.
  • Hard Drive: Bill Gates and the Making of the Microsoft Empire by James Wallace & Jim Erikson - A detailed account of Bill Gates' journey and the rise of Microsoft.
  • iWoz: Computer Geek to Cult Icon by Steve Wozniak - The story of Apple's co-founder and his contributions to the personal computing revolution.
  • Elon Musk: Tesla, SpaceX, and the Quest for a Fantastic Future by Ashlee Vance - An exploration of the life and ambitions of one of today's most influential tech entrepreneurs.
  • Idea Man: A Memoir of the Cofounder of Microsoft by Paul Allen - Insights into the life of Microsoft co-founder Paul Allen and his impact on technology.
